This build costs about **$82.19 CAD**. Prices vary — watch for AliExpress "Choice" discounts when buying 3+ items.

## Required Components

- **$19.90** Radio & MCU: [Xiao NRF52840 w/ Wio (SX1262)](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005008760784706.html)
- **$32.30** Antenna: [Gizont "8" dBi Fiberglass outdoor antenna](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005005132142342.html)
- **$6.30** Pigtail: [N Female to IPX/UFL RG178 Cable](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005001920963497.html)
- **$7.19** Enclosure: [ABS Waterproof Box 100×68×50mm](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005005728138924.html)
- **$9.00** Solar Panel: [10W 5V Solar Panel Micro Type-C](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005008564961293.html)
- **$10.00** Battery: Any 3.7V Li-Ion cell or pack
- **$3.34** Buck Converter: [DC 5.5–27V to 3.3/5/12V MP1584](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005006976822556.html)
